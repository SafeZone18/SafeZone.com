Hello & Welcome to Safe Zone

Hi my name is Emma I am the creator of this website.

It is to help you with any problems relating with being bullied.

Also if you are the bully it is also going to help you with things that are going on in your life.

It doesn't matter if you are an adult or a child this is for you to use, and to help you not feel so alone.

I know that it does get hard for some people to relate to others that have somewhat the same thing that had or is happing to them.

Please try at least a week or two on here to see if you like it and if you don't that is also ok not everything is meant for everyone.

Just because somethings might not help you right away or it gets to hard in life for you don't give up no matter if that happens.

If I did that every time I would get nowhere in life and this website would never get done.

The one thing that I will get the most is if you are not ready to talk or get out of the shell that you are in that is okay.

You know what you want and are capable of.

But if you are ready to do that then try it and see what happens.
